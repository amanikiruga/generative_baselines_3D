Notes & caveats

  • Checkpoint: latest stable mixture epoch (step 2850). The newer last.ckpt was being actively rewritten by the in-flight training run when this BFS pass started, so we pinned the prior fully-written epoch checkpoint to avoid mid-load truncation.
  • RE10K depth: dataset has no GT depth — depth metrics for our method on RE10K are intentionally not reported (we inject zero-depth at inference so the FLF VAE chunking works; pose & NVS predictions remain valid).
  • GEN3C: env-blocked in test2 (transformer_engine API mismatch — apply_rotary_pos_emb no longer exported). Add to results once env is rebuilt.
  • Pending datasets (T&T, Sintel, KITTI, ETH3D, DTU): data not on disk in the form needed for pose/depth eval. Sintel-depth zip and KITTI-Eigen need to be downloaded; T&T needs camera intrinsics; ETH3D dir is empty. Loaders will be written once data lands.
  • Sample budget: 10 scenes per (dataset, task) — BFS pass; not statistically tight. RE10K test_percentage caps at 656 candidates, DL3DV at 63, Scannet++ at 7. We intersect with --max_samples 10.
  • Reproducing: full commands in README_here.md. Output base eval_outputs/ is a symlink to /n/netscratch/ydu_lab/Everyone/akiruga/generative_baselines_eval/ (the holyoke ydu_lab volume hit 100% mid-run).
